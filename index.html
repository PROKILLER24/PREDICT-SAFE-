PRECAUTION'S DO'S AND DONT'S

DO'S Drop, Cover, and Hold On: During shaking, drop to your hands and knees, cover your head and neck, and hold on to sturdy furniture.

DO'S Move to Higher Ground: If there is no evacuation order but you are in a flood-prone area, move to higher ground. Go to safe palce and stay away from water.

DO'S Secure Property: Board up windows, secure outdoor objects, and reinforce doors. Evacuate if Instructed.

DO's Prepare an Emergency Kit: Include essentials like water, non-perishable food, flashlight, batteries, first-aid supplies, medications, important documents, and cash.

Don’t Use Open Flames:Avoid using candles or open flames if there's a risk of gas leaks or power outages. Use flashlights instead.

Don’t Use Elevators : Avoid using elevators during a natural disaster, particularly during earthquakes or floods, as power outages or damage can leave you trapped.

Don’t Panic: Stay calm and think clearly. Panicking can lead to poor decision-making.

Don’t Spread Misinformation : Only share information from reliable sources. Misinformation can cause unnecessary panic and confusion.
